 Features of Ultimaker 2+ and Ultimaker 2 Extended+

1. Swappable Nozzle

Ultimaker 2+ allows you to easily switch between 0.25, 0.4, 0.6, 0.8 mm nozzles. So, how does it help? If you want the printed object to have high precise details, 0.4 mm nozzle is preferred which goes till 20 microns. Want to make faster prototypes? Swap with the 0.8mm nozzle and it can go up to 600 microns.

 



2. Geared Feeder

Ultimaker 2+ features the Geared feeder which allows you to easily swap filament and choose the correct pressure for your filament. For example, with a softer filament, youll need to choose a lower pressure and vice versa for a harder filament. Such control increases the success rate of your prints.
